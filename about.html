About Quranesia Online Academy

Quranesia Online Academy is dedicated to delivering high-quality Quran education to students all over the world. Our mission is to spread the light of the Quran by making Quranic learning accessible, affordable, and effective for people of all ages.

We offer comprehensive online courses including Hifz, Tajweed, Qaida, and Islamic Studies, guided by qualified and experienced teachers. Our flexible timing and personalized approach ensure every student receives focused attention.

Whether you are a beginner or looking to enhance your recitation skills, Quranesia Online Academy is here to help you every step of the way. Join us in this blessed journey of learning the Book of Allah.
