About Quranesia Academy

Quranesia Online Academy is an educational platform dedicated to spreading the message of the Quran globally. Founded by Qari Muhammad Waseem, our mission is to make Quran education accessible, affordable, and effective for all ages.

  • Expert male & female teachers
  • Courses in Noorani Qaida, Tajweed, Hifz, and Islamic Studies
  • One-on-one and group classes via Zoom, WhatsApp, and Google Meet
  • Flexible timing for kids, adults, and women
  • Free trial classes for every new student
