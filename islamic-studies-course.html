Islamic Studies Course

Our Islamic Studies course is designed to provide students with foundational and advanced knowledge of the Islamic way of life. The course is ideal for children, youth, and adults who wish to learn more about Deen, Akhlaq, Fiqh, and the Seerah of the Prophet (SAW).

  • Basic Islamic Beliefs (Aqaid)
  • Seerah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • Introduction to Fiqh and Islamic rulings
  • Good Manners and Akhlaq
  • Daily Islamic Duas and Sunnah Practices
